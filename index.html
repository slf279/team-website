Protecting forest elephants

Through this project, we intend to educate the public about the threat of forest elephant poaching and its devastating effects on the the rainforest environments of the Congo Basin and on global warming. Our project will also provide ways for the public to help fight back against forest elephant poaching by offering donation opportunities to accredited elephant-centric anti-poaching organizations.
